# Epic 3: Multimedia Capabilities

## Overview
Enhance the chat experience by allowing users to share images and audio for better symptom analysis.

## User Stories

### US3.1: Image Input from Camera/Gallery
**As a** user,
**I want** to take photos of my symptoms (e.g., rash, wound) or upload existing ones,
**So that** the AI can visually analyze the condition.

### US3.2: Voice Input (Speech-to-Text)
**As a** user,
**I want** to speak my symptoms instead of typing,
**So that** I can describe my condition more naturally and quickly.

### US3.3: Audio Input (Symptom Recording)
**As a** user,
**I want** to record sounds like coughing or breathing,
**So that** the AI can analyze auditory symptoms.

### US3.4: Multimedia Storage Management
**As a** user,
**I want** to manage (view/delete) the media files associated with my chats,
**So that** I maintain control over my personal health data.

## Progress

- ✅ US3.1: Image attachment from camera/gallery implemented; messages can include image URIs and display properly in chat.
- 🟡 US3.2 / US3.3: Audio capture infrastructure exists but transcription and analysis flows are still TODO.
- 🟡 US3.4: Basic deletion UI present; further polish needed for media management screen.
